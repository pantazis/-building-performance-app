Our mission

OpenBEP helps teams measure building performance with transparent and practical workflows. We combine IFC-based data, domain scoring, and reporting into one clear process.

Demo focus: faster onboarding, consistent assessments, and actionable reports.

What we deliver

  • IFC import and validation
  • KPI-based domain analysis
  • EPB and SRI-ready outputs
  • Optional API integrations
